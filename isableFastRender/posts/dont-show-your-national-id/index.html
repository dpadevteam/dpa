Do Not Show The Back Of Your National ID to Anyone

By Digital Privacy Advocacy

February 27, 2024

In light of research conducted by a cybersecurity firm in the Philippines, it has been discovered that both the physical and digital versions of the Philippine Identification System ID, or more commonly known as the National ID, have critical security flaws that can allow potential theft and fraud.
