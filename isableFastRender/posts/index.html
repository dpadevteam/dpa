What is Privacy

According to Dictionary.com, privacy is: the state of being free from unwanted or undue intrusion or disturbance in one’s private life or affairs; freedom to be let alone. More simply, privacy means having control over who knows information about you and what information they know. For example, when you go to the bathroom, you close the door because you want to have privacy. You don’t want anyone else to see you using the toilet.
